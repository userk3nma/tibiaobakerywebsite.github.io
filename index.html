The Great Story

Tibiao Bakery Journey

Tibiao Bakery is a beloved institution in the philippines, known for its traditional baking methods known for itstraditional baking methods and fresh, delicious bread and pastries. They use both a wood-fired oven and modern techniques to create a varied menu that includes pandesal, ensaymada, biscocho, otap, and catalogan.

The bakery has expanded to IloIlo City, but remains true to its roots in Tibiao, Antique, where they still use their traditional wood-fired oven. They are committed to their community, sourcing ingredients locally and providing jobs to local people. Every bite of their food feels like a taste of home, with a piece of history attached.
